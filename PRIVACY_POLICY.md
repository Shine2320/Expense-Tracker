# Privacy Policy - Expense Tracker

**Last Updated:** May 3, 2026

## Overview
Expense Tracker is a personal expense tracking application that operates entirely offline. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection
**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

- No internet connection is required or used
- No analytics or tracking SDKs are included
- No data is transmitted to any external servers
- No advertising or third-party SDKs are used

## Data Storage
All data is stored **locally on your device** using Hive, a fast NoSQL local database:

- **Expenses** - Your expense records (name, amount, date, category)
- **Categories** - Your expense categories and preferences
- **Settings** - Your monthly salary and balance settings

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Cannot be accessed by us or any third party
- Is automatically removed when you uninstall the app
- Is not backed up to cloud services

## Permissions
This app requests **no sensitive permissions**. It only uses standard Android permissions required to run on your device.

## Data Security
Since all data is stored locally:
- Android's app sandbox isolates your data from other apps
- Data is deleted when you uninstall the app
- No server-side data means no risk of data breaches

## Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.
